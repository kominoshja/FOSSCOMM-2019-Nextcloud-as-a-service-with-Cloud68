Back in 1989

Simpler times

Computers ran code, and served it to the users that wanted to see the output

But there was a problem

Who was going to pay for these computers?

The developers couldn't afford them

And the user didn't want to pay for each site that they would access

And so, a "solution" was found

Sites were going to be free to access, granted that you had an internet connection

And the developers needed to make money in some other way

ADVERTISMENTS

And therefore, you'd make more money
